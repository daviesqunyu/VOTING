How Our AI Secures Your Vote

Our system uses a combination of cloud security layers, blockchain, and artificial intelligence to secure Kenyans' votes. Each ballot is cryptographically sealed and verified through a decentralized network, making it impossible to alter or tamper with results.

  3. Cast your vote through our encrypted platform
  4. Verify your vote was counted correctly using your token
